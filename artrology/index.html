Self-Help Guide : Artrology

This is a self-help document based on the astrology of art whose 11 signs appear in 11 snippets of barcodes. The guide predicts and advises on the artistic affairs about presentation and discourse around you and your practice, as well as general career from the next few weeks to months.

The basis of Artrology lies on the cusp of the observational study in art events, severe inexperience, arbitrary wishes, wishful seriousness, personal lack of assurance, and relative positions of roles, words, and practices. Phase into the doc with strokes of names or your volatility-of-creativity index.
